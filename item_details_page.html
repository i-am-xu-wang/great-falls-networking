About Bird Watchers
Welcome to the Bird Watchers! If you are a nature lover who wants to get out with like-minded people for a day of adventure and fun while seeing and learning about nature. Then this is the group for you. Can you imagine the thrill of a Bald Eagle soaring over your head? It's an unbelievable feeling! Seeing your first hummingbird in the wild is a moment that you would cherish for a lifetime! We will be travelling inside different parts of Great Falls for bird watching adventures. We only have small groups for a more intimate experience.
